About Us

RCC is a youth-led reading team initiated since the 16th September 2016 in Siem Reap, Cambodia.

Our Mission is to Engage and garner youths within local communities to develop healthy reading habits and conduct weekend reading activities + sharing sessions. Create events that reinforces the power and importance of reading.

Our Vision Youths within our community display the vast outcomes of strong and well-maintained habits of reading. Youths continue to drive positive impacts beyond their own community.

About our Founder

Brian Synak Ork, a passionate educator and visionary who established Read Club Cambodia in September 2016. He founded the club with a deep commitment to closing the literacy gap in Cambodia, particularly focusing on children in rural areas who lack access to books and educational materials.

As a passionate educator and visionary, Brian Synak Ork founded Read Club Cambodia to cultivate a love for reading among Cambodian children. With a deep commitment to education and literacy, he recognized the significant literacy gap in Cambodia, where many children in rural areas lack access to books and educational materials. Inspired by the transformative power of books, Brian established Read Club Cambodia in September 2016, to provide children with the tools and opportunities to develop their reading skills and imagination.

Motivated by his recognition of the literacy gap, Brian envisioned a platform that would foster a culture of reading and empower children with knowledge, critical thinking skills, and confidence. He aimed to create an engaging and supportive environment for young readers, inspiring them to pursue their dreams. Through Read Club Cambodia, Brian continues to make a lasting impact on the educational landscape of Cambodia, uplifting the next generation and empowering them to achieve their full potential.
